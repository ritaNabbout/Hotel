Asian Food

Breakfast

Vietnamese Bahn Mi Op La

fried egg in a delicious crispy small baguette, with a little chili sauce. Wash it down with some fresh juice and a ca phe sua da (Vietnamese iced coffee).

Japanese Omurice

Omelette made with fried rice and thin, fried scrambled eggs, usually topped with ketchup.

Filipino Tamales

Tamales made of rice flour, shredded chicken, and salted duck egg. They're wrapped in banana leaves and steamed. Pair it with a glass of carabao milk and slices of ripe mango.

Chinese Jianbing

Savory Crepe. It can be topped with different fillings and sauces such as baocui (薄脆, thin and crispy fried cracker), ham, chopped or diced mustard pickles, scallions and coriander, chili sauce or hoisin sauce

Malaysian Roti Canai

Crispy on the outside yet so fluffy on the inside.erved with variants of curry (such as dhal, chicken, etc) as well as a dollop of sambal.

Masala Dosa

made by soaking rice and lentils in water overnight before blending into a batter. It’s then stuffed with cooked potatoes spiced using methi (or fenugreek as it’s also known) and curry leaves and served alongside chutneys and sambar. Sambar is a lentil-based stew or chowder cooked with tamarind.

Dan bing

Crêpe with a beaten egg or two and your desired toppings, Spring onions, chicken and basil, bacon and cheese, tuna mayonnaise and even fried chicken.

Miso Soup And More

steamed rice, a bowl of miso soup, a selection of pickled vegetables, a Japanese-style omelette and a side of natto. Natto is a strong-smelling, polarising dish made from fermented soybeans.

Lunch

Spicy Baked Tofu

Tofu is a concentrated protein, so you don't need as much tofu as you would meat.Served this with wilted garlic spinach and a fresh salad on the side.

Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls

Vietnamese dish traditionally consisting of pork, prawn, vegetables, bún (rice vermicelli), and other ingredients wrapped in rice paper.

Chinese Spareribs

Pork spare ribs may be fried, steamed, or braised.

Chinese Green Bean Stir-Fry

Beans stir-fried with garlic and douchi.

Keema Aloo

Ground Beef & Potato Curry.Served with some crunchy vegetables like red onions or cucumber and bread.

Machhere Jhol

Very spicy stew or gravy that is served with rice seasoned with turmeric, garlic, onions, and grated ginger and Indian spices with potatoes and tomatoes.

Ash-e-jow

Soup of beans and grains with an abundance of fresh herbs and spinach. The stew is topped with Kashk , tender crispy fried onions, and a garlic mint sauce.

The Best Thai Coconut Soup

Coconut soup is sometimes prepared in combinations using other ingredients, such as chicken, potato, or curry.It can be served as a hot or cold soup.

Dinner

Baozi

light and fluffy buns come stuffed with a range of delicious fillings, from barbecue-flavoured char sui pork to sweet egg custard.

Tteokbokki

Eomuk (fish cakes), boiled eggs, and scallions are some common ingredients paired with tteokbokki in dishes. It can be seasoned with either spicy gochujang (chili paste) or non-spicy ganjang (soy sauce)-based sauce.

Tempura Batter

Consisting of seafood, meat, and vegetables that have been battered and deep fried.

Better-Than-Takeout Beef and Broccoli

This Chinese beef and broccoli is full of umami flavor and pairs nicely with steamed rice or cauliflower rice.

Korean Fried Chicken

Basic huraideu-chicken, crunchy exterior and its thick sweet-and-spicy sauce.

Asian Sloppy Joes

Full of ground pork, hoisin sauce, ginger, chili garlic sauce, and more!

Dumpling Stir Fry

Sumpling stir-fry recipe was born out of my love and devotion to a certain chili sauce .vegetable dumplings – with a whole lot of veggies and very little time.

Rainbow Stir-Fry

Noodles with onions, carrots, broccoli, peppers, and green beans

Sushi

Temaki

rolling nori sheets into cone shapes, and then filling them with fish, veggies, and rice.

Maki

cut-rolled sushi , made using seaweed thats wrapped with a layer of veggies, rice and fish.

Sashimi

The fish is sliced in long and rectangular slices. it’s served alongside soy sauce and ginger.

Nigiri

Sushi rice balls seasoned with rice vinegar , and then topped with a slice of raw fish.

About The Asian Food


This is how we roll!

With Passion For Real, Good Food

Asian cuisine includes several major regional cuisines: Central Asian, East Asian, North Asian, South Asian, Southeast Asian, and West Asian.Ingredients common to many cultures in the East and Southeast regions of the continent include rice, ginger, garlic, sesame seeds, chilies, dried onions, soy, and tofu. Stir frying, steaming, and deep frying are common cooking methods.
