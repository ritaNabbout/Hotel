Lunch

Coq au vin

chicken braised with wine, mushrooms, salty pork or bacon (lardons), mushrooms, onions, garlic and sometimes even a drop of brandy.

Boeuf bourguignon

is essentially a stew made from beef braised in red wine, beef broth, and seasoned vegetables including pearl onions and mushrooms.

Confit de canard

duck meat marinated in salt, garlic, and thyme for around 36 hours and then slow-cooked in its own fat at low temperatures.It is typically served with confit roasted potatoes and garlic on the side.

Ratatouille

Vegetables shallow-fried and then layered in a casserole dish before being baked in an oven.

Escargots de Bourgogne

This recipe is cooked with a whole lot of butter, garlic, and parsley.

Foie gras

French foie gras with apple and pear chutney on the toast.

The salad Jaco

pan-fried scallops in olive oil with a green salad. The addition of caramelized onions brought a little sweetness to the vinaigrette-tossed greens.

Spinach & Mushrooms Quiche

Homestyle pastry crust filled with egg, cream, spinach and mushrooms. Served with baby greens with french vinaigrette.

Mussels

East-coast blue mussels cooked in a shallot and white wine garlic broth. Served with a house paprika-lemon aioli on a French baguette.

Escabèche d'écrevisses sur gaspacho d'asperge et cresson

This dish consists of marinated crayfish on gazpacho asparagus and watercress.

Blanquette de Veau

Blanquette is usually served with rice à la créole but may also be served with celeriac (celery root), halved celery hearts, carrots, braised parsnips or leeks, cucumber (cut into chunks and braised three minutes in boiling salted water, braised lettuce or lettuce hearts.

Sous vide fillet of beef, pomme anna and mushroom purée

prime cut of beef with a mushroom purée, pomme Anna potatoes and celeriac.

Dinner

French Onion Soup

based on meat stock and onions, and often served gratinéed with croutons or a larger piece of bread covered with cheese floating on top. Served with fresh toast.

Salmon En Papillote

Salmon fillets placed on a bed of vegetables, wrapped in a parchment paper pouch (en papillote) and baked in the oven. The fish is also topped with basil compound butter for extra flavor!

Croque Monsieur

French ham and cheese sandwich made with gruyere, parmesan, ham and a simple béchamel sauce, toasted in the oven.Serve with a green salad and a small cup of French Onion Soup and you have the perfect Parisian meal!

Lamb Shank Navarin

Lamb in the sauce with potato, turnip, parsley,seasoned with salt and pepper

Lyonnaise Potatoes

Pan-fried potato with the addition of caramelized onions and lots of butter.

Salade Aveyronnaise

Bacon and Roquefort cheese as its main ingredients, including garlic, basil, and baby spinach dressed with a vinaigrette based on sherry vinegar and mild-tasting olive oil,topped with even more cheese and nuts

Chicken Cordon Bleu Quesadillas

Cheesy, savory goodness in every crispy bite!Swiss cheese, skinless chicken breasts, dijon mustard, flour tortillas, olive oil.

Chicken Roulade

Chicken Roulade Stuffed with Brussels Sprouts, Walnuts, and Pecorino.Warm up with this nutty, cheesy, earthy take on stuffed chicken.
