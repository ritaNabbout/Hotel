The rice crisps are optional, so if they seem like a bridge too far, opt for coarsely chopped salted peanuts instead. Begin this recipe a day ahead to dry out the jasmine rice and soak the black rice.

Kalimantan Banana

Fried bananas,presented in different ways from banana fritters to slender fried planks.

Bubar Ayam

Lunch

Satay

Grilled meats served with spicy sauce. The meat usually consists of diced or sliced chicken, goat, mutton, beef and pork, but you'll also find satay that's made with fish, tofu, eggs or minced blends.

Nasi ayam and nasi campur

plate of white rice comes with different elements of Balinese food, such as a portion of babi guling (roast suckling pig) or betutu (spiced chicken or duck), mixed vegetables, and a dab of spicy sambal matah (Balinese sauce).

Bebek and ayam betutu

Roast suckling pig,with traditional spices, wrapped in banana leaves, then enveloped tight in the bark of a banana trunk.

Babi Guling

spit-roast pig stuffed with rich traditional spices and vegetable mixes such as cassava leaves, slowly rolled over (guling means "to roll" in Indonesian) a coal fire.

Tahu and tempeh

Tahu (tofu) and tempeh come in different preparations – some as savoury snacks, and others as accompaniments and main-course dishes. These soybean curds can be fried, stuffed and battered.

Pepes And Tum

Pepes uses banana leaf as food wrappings.fish as pepes ikan, but meat, chicken, tofu or vegetables are common ingredients as well.Tum commonly contains minced pork mixed with spiced paste.

Nasi Goreng

Stir-Frying cooked rice with a combination of meats and vegetables, ranging from scrambled eggs, diced beef, strips of chicken, shrimp, anchovies, lamb, crab, green peas, onions, shallots and a blend of sweet soy sauce and hot chilli sauce.

Mie Goreng

Fried noodles is often served with vegetables and a choice of chicken, shrimp or pork. Sometimes it served with a fried egg on top, chicken sate sticks, prawn crackers and peanut sauce. Oh, and don’t forget the pickled vegetables.

Mini Rijsttafel

smoked duck, chicken with sambal, prawns, pork & chicken sates, sayur urap (mixed Bali vegetables), potato croquettes, tempe, tofu and yellow nasi tumpeng (rice cone).

Spring Rolls

Filled with mixed vegetables and minced chicken, served with a peanut sauce or a sweet chilli sauce.

Gado-Gado

Vegetable salad bathed in a classic peanut sauce.At its base are boiled long beans, spinach, potato, corn, egg and bean sprouts coupled with cucumber, tofu and tempe.

Lawar

is made by mixing chopped meat with various green vegetables and grated coconut. As for the meat, the choices are either beef, chicken, duck, pork, turtle, or a combination of those.

Eggs With Traditional Balinese Flavor

Boiling whole eggs with guava leaves, shallot skin and garlic skin, until they turn a deep, dark brown color. Then, the eggs are cooked again in an aromatic Balinese spice paste.

Rujak

Fruit and vegetable salad that is served with a thick sauce made of chilli and peanuts.It includes water apple, raw unripe mango, pineapple, cucumber, or sweet potato
