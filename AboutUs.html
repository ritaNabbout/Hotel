    Nyepi day

    Unlike typical celebrations of the new year all over the world which marked with endless fireworks shows, Balinese prefer to celebrate their first day of the year by dedicating a full day to connect with God (Hyang Widi Wasa) through prayer, fasting and meditation. During Nyepi, all the people on the island are not allowed to go out of their house. Every road is closed down, even the airport and harbour will be shut down for a whole day.
